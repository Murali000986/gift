=========================================================
      🦋 THE ENCHANTED FOREST - A GIFT FOR SHRESTA 🦋
=========================================================

ABOUT THIS PROJECT
------------------
"The Enchanted Forest" is a custom-built, interactive, magical storybook website created specifically for Shresta. 
It is designed not just to be a website, but an emotional journey. It uses advanced web animations, physics-based particles, 3D CSS effects, and ambient synthesizer music to create a deeply immersive experience.

The core message of this gift is to remind Shresta of her own inner strength, beauty, and magic. She must actively explore the forest, clicking on hidden objects to uncover words of encouragement and love.

KEY FEATURES & MECHANICS
------------------------
- Interactive Discovery: Shresta must click hidden items (leaves, flowers, butterflies, stars) on each page to progress.
- Progress Saving: Progress is saved as she plays. However, visiting the Entrance page (home page) automatically wipes the slate clean, allowing her to restart the magical journey completely fresh at any time!
- Cinematic Atmosphere: Ambient soundscapes generate in real-time, accompanied by weather effects, moving fog, interactive mouse-particles, and glowing elements.


THE 8 CHAPTERS OF THE JOURNEY
-----------------------------

1. THE ENTRANCE 🌙
The journey begins at night under a twinkling sky with moving parallax clouds and glowing fireflies. A magical butterfly flutters down to welcome her.
*Function*: Visiting this page resets the entire game to a fresh state so she can play through again manually.
Message: "Every journey begins with a single step."

2. THE TREE OF MEMORIES 🌳
A giant, softly swaying oak tree stands in the darkness. Hidden within its branches are 5 secret birds and leaves. When she finds them all, a hidden glowing branch grows to point her forward.
Hidden Messages:
- "You are stronger than you think. You have survived things that once seemed impossible."
- "Your voice matters. Never let anyone silence the music inside you."
- "Some battles are invisible... You are brave for fighting them."
- "Growth takes time, just like trees. Be patient with yourself."
- "Even falling leaves are beautiful. Change is not an ending — it's a becoming."

3. THE GARDEN OF DREAMS 🌸
A beautiful garden where glowing pink petals fall from the sky and follow her mouse pointer. She must click the glowing flowers to bloom them. There is also a secret, fast-running bunny (🐇) she can catch!
Hidden Messages:
- "Dreams are not silly. They are the most honest thing about you."
- "Keep creating. Every brushstroke, every word, every idea — it matters."
- "Your future self is so proud of you for not giving up today."
- "You bloom at your own pace. Never compare your chapter 1 to someone else's chapter 20."
- "You deserve all the beauty you pour into the world."

4. THE SKY ISLANDS ☁️
She ascends into the clouds where magical islands float. Birds fly in formation in the background. Finding all the islands bridges the gap with a magical animated rainbow.
Hidden Messages:
- "Look how high you've climbed. Don't forget to appreciate the view."
- "Sometimes you have to let go of the ground to see how far you can fly."
- "Your perspective is unique. The world needs the way you see it."
- "Peace is not a place you find, it's a space you create within yourself."
- "You are not falling. You are learning how to soar."

5. BUTTERFLY LAKE 🦋
A serene, deeply reflective lake mirrors the night sky. Glowing butterflies fly across the screen using organic pathfinding. When she clicks them, they freeze and transform into glowing Memory Crystals, illuminating the lake.
Hidden Messages:
- "Every tear you've cried was just watering the soil for your next beautiful chapter."
- "You don't have to be perfect to be worthy of love."
- "Take a deep breath. You are exactly where you are supposed to be right now."
- "Your sensitivity is not a weakness. It is your superpower."

6. CONSTELLATION SKY ⭐
A gorgeous starry night sky. She must click specific glowing stars to connect the lines and draw 5 constellations (Courage, Hope, Kindness, Dreams, Strength). 
Once all 5 are drawn, the stars magically rearrange themselves, accompanied by shooting stars, to spell her name: "✨ SHRESTA ✨"

7. THE SUNRISE FINALE 🌅
The grand conclusion of the main journey. The sky transitions through 4 phases (Night -> Dawn -> Sunrise -> Golden Morning). The tree blossoms with pink flowers, all the butterflies gather, and a heartfelt letter types itself out on the screen:

"Dear Shresta,
Life won't always be easy.
There will be nights that feel endless, storms that feel unfair, and battles that are invisible to everyone but you.
But just like this forest — even after the darkest night, flowers bloom again.
You are not alone. You are seen. You are enough. You are loved.
Keep going, beautiful soul. Your story is still being written — and it is magnificent."

After the letter finishes, a glowing button appears, pointing to the final hidden chapter: "💎 The Healing Glass →"

8. THE HEALING GLASS 💎 (The Secret Epilogue)
A deeply emotional, cinematic interactive experience set in a dark magical atmosphere with floating particles and drifting fog.
Phase 1: A glowing crystal heart appears.
Phase 2: It shatters into 7 pieces in slow motion, surrounded by a surround-sound glass cracking effect.
Phase 3: The 7 fragments float in zero gravity. Shresta must catch each one. Catching a fragment plays a unique animation (butterflies, flowers, raining petals, etc.) and reveals a powerful quote about healing.
Phase 4: The pieces reassemble into a beautiful Kintsugi golden heart (broken but beautifully healed).
Phase 5: Fun, interactive 3D Flip Cards quiz Shresta on playful memories.
Phase 6: A final magical choice where she decides the future, triggering a gorgeous transition to pure white with 80 stars and the final words: "🦋 The End 🦋"


TECHNICAL DETAILS
-----------------
- Framework: React + TypeScript + Vite
- Animations: Framer Motion (for UI/SVG/3D Flips) + Custom HTML5 Canvas (for high-performance particles/stars/butterflies)
- State: Zustand (Progress is tracked but wipes completely fresh when visiting the Entrance page)
- Audio: Web Audio API (Synthesizing ambient, soothing frequencies that cross-fade between pages)
